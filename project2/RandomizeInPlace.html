Randomize In Place

This algorithm randomizes an originally sorted array in place. Click step forward to see the algorithm and corresponding pseudocode select the next index and swap it with a random index. Click restart to restart the animation.

for i = 1 to A.length:
    swap A[i] with A[Random(i,A.length)]
